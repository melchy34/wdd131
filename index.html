Introduction to Web Development

Web development refers to the process of creating and maintaining websites. It involves a combination of activities, including web design, coding, content creation, and server-side configuration. Web development can range from building a simple static webpage to creating complex dynamic applications. The field has evolved over the years with new technologies and frameworks emerging, making it an exciting and ever-changing domain.

Advanced Web Techniques

As the field of web development continues to evolve, developers have access to more advanced techniques and tools that allow for the creation of dynamic, interactive, and high-performance websites and applications.
